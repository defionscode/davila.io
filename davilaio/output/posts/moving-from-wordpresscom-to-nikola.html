I've Moved

This is a simple post. I've decided to use Nikola + GitHub Pages to do my blogging instead of using WordPress.com. The reason is straightforward; the friendliness of doing technical writeups. In wordpress.com there is no support for writing posts in markdown and codeblocks are a glorious pain to use and require a constant check of the 'preview' to produce something that looks decent. In a subsequent post I'll write out how I made the transtion and few minor obstacles that came out of it. The old blog, should you desire to take a look can be ~~found here~~.
